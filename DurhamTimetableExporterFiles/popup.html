Timetable Exporter
  1. Navigate to the Teaching Timetable
  2. Choose your modules as usual
  3. Don't change any other settings
  4. Click 'View Timetable'
  5. 'Download Timetable' link will appear
  6. Import the downloaded file into your calendar program (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, Yahoo, Mozilla Thunderbird...etc)
It is advised you make a separate calendar for your timetable as there will be a lot of events
